About

I’m a junior Full-Stack Developer with a background in landscape gardening and marketing. After moving down to London in January 2020 in search of an entry-level sales role I found my career search halted by Covid-19. With entry level roles drying up I made the decision in June to move back to my home village and return to a self-employed landscape gardener role, of which I have 5+ years experience. After a month or so of brainstorming career options I landed on the idea of becoming a software developer and thus signed on to the CareerFoundry Full-Stack Developer course. This wasn’t totally out the blue, as I previously created a Wix website for a local store and also a travel photography portfolio for myself.
